How It Works


When you’re getting ready to head out, whether you’re treating yourself to something special, or going out with friends, you can use the GoDiner App to make reservations, check menus, read reviews and even decide on which venue you want to go to by popular vote.


Now you arrive at your chosen destination...



Table Service


By connecting to your table via Beacon technology, the GoDiner app knows which venue’s menu to load and what table you are sitting at – so the staff know exactly where you are for service. You may now order what you want, when you want it or call a waiter directly to your table. Total control is now in your hands!

No Table Service? No Problem!


If a GoDiner venue does not offer the beacon table service feature, you can still open a tab and place orders. With no table service offered – when your order has been prepared, staff will send you a “Pick Up” notification with your tab number to present upon collection. You can continue to run your tab and simply pay the bill when you are ready to leave. No more queues!

App Features

Seamless Ordering

Customers can now order directly through to the venue in their own time frame by connecting to their table and completely controlling their experience.

Social Reservations

Is a unique function that facilitates a vote between friends on where and when a reservation is made, making organising an occasion of any size, quick and easy.

Call for Service

Should a customer require attention, a simple touch of the 'Table Service' button on the GoDiner App will send a service request to notify staff for the need of attention. This feature resembles that found on in-flight services.

Wait Time Estimates

The GoDiner App also offers a wait time estimate of how long it may take to prepare your order. This is perfect for pre-ordering and pick-ups.

Push Notifications

By simply selecting a GoDiner venue as one of your favorites, you instantly become eligible to receive special offers, discounts or news about upcoming events.

Store Payment Receipts

GoDiner has a built-in receipt book that allows you to keep track of all in-app payments in one place.
